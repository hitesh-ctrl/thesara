Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

📖

Instant Definitions

Hover any word to see its definition, part of speech, and synonyms — without leaving the screen.

🧠

Contextual AI Meaning

Understands what words mean in that scene. "Cook" in Breaking Bad isn't about food.

🔗

Idiom Detection

Automatically detects phrases like "kick the bucket" or "give up" and explains them as a unit.

⚡

Zero Setup

Install and it works. No account. No configuration. No API key required to get started.

🔒

Private by Design

Only the subtitle line you hover is sent to AI. No history, no account data, no tracking.

🆓

Free Forever

Shared AI quota provided by the developer at no cost. Bring your own Groq key if the daily quota runs out.

How it works

Three seconds to understand any word

01

Watch normally

Thesara runs silently in the background. Your viewing experience is completely uninterrupted.

02

Hover a word

Move your mouse over any subtitle word. The video pauses and a tooltip appears instantly.

03

Understand it

See the definition, synonyms, and exactly what the word means in this scene.

04

Keep watching

Close the tooltip and the video resumes right where you left off.
