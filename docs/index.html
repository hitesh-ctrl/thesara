Features

Built for serious learners

📖

Instant Definitions

Definition, part of speech, and synonyms — on hover, without leaving the screen.

🧠

Scene-Aware AI

"Cook" in Breaking Bad isn't about food. Thesara knows the show, the character, and the moment.

🔗

Idiom Detection

Automatically clusters phrases like "call it a day" and explains them as a single unit.

💬

Dialogue Context

The AI sees the last 4 lines of dialogue — so it understands the conversation, not just the sentence.

🔒

Private by Design

Only the subtitle you hover ever leaves your device. No history. No tracking. No accounts.

⚡

Zero Config

Install and it works. No API key needed to start. Shared quota provided free by the developer.

How it works

Four steps, three seconds

01

Watch normally

Thesara runs silently. Your viewing is completely uninterrupted.

02

Hover a word

Move your cursor over any subtitle word or detected phrase.

03

Read the tooltip

Definition, synonyms, and the AI's scene-aware explanation appear instantly.

04

Keep watching

Dismiss the tooltip and the video resumes exactly where you left off.
