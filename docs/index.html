Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

01 · Dictionary

Instant definitions

Definition, part of speech, and synonyms on hover, without leaving the screen or breaking focus.

02 · AI

Scene-aware context

"Cook" in Breaking Bad isn't about food. The AI reads the show, the characters, and the last 6 lines of dialogue.

03 · NLP

Idiom detection

Automatically detects phrases like "call it a day" and explains them as a single unit, not word by word.

04 · Quota

Free shared key. Bring your own.

A shared Groq API key is provided free for all users, up to 14,400 calls per day. When the daily quota runs out, you're shown a clear disclosure and can plug in your own free Groq key to keep going. No surprise charges, ever.
