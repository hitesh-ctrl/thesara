Features

Built for how
you actually watch.

01

Instant definitions

Hover any subtitle word for its definition, part of speech, and synonyms — without leaving the screen.

02

Scene-aware AI

"Cook" in Breaking Bad isn't about food. The AI knows the show, the scene, and the moment — and explains accordingly.

03

Idiom detection

Automatically identifies phrases like "call it a day" or "give it up" and explains them as a single unit of meaning.

04

Dialogue context

The last 6 subtitle lines are passed to the AI, so it understands the conversation — not just the sentence you hovered.

05

Private by design

Only the subtitle you hover ever leaves your device. No history, no profiling, no accounts required.

06

Zero configuration

Install and it works. No API key needed to start. A shared quota is provided free. Bring your own key if it runs out.
