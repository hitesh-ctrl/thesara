Features

Built for how you actually watch

📖

Instant Definitions

Hover any word for its definition, part of speech, and synonyms without leaving the screen or pausing your flow.

🧠

Scene-Aware AI

"Cook" in Breaking Bad isn't about food. Thesara understands what words mean in context — the show, the character, the scene.

🔗

Idiom Detection

Automatically groups phrases like "kick the bucket" or "give it a shot" and explains them as a single unit.

⚡

Zero Setup

Install and it works immediately. No account. No configuration. No API key required to get started.

🔒

Private by Design

Only the subtitle you hover is sent for lookup. No viewing history, no account data, no tracking of any kind.

🆓

Genuinely Free

Shared AI quota provided by the developer at no cost. Add your own free Groq key if the daily quota runs out.

How it works

Three seconds to understand any word

01

Watch normally

Thesara runs silently in the background. Your viewing is completely uninterrupted.

02

Hover a word

Move your mouse over any subtitle word or detected phrase.

03

Read the tooltip

Definition, synonyms, and the AI's scene-aware explanation appear instantly.

04

Keep watching

Close the tooltip and the video resumes exactly where you left off.
